Hungary is a medium-sized, landlocked central European country covering an area of 93,030 km2(35,920 sq mi).

Flag

The flag of Hungary is a horizontal tricolor flag of red, white, and green.

The red of Hungary's flag is said to stand for strength or bloodshed (in battle), the white for faithfulness or freedom, and the green for hope or the green fields of Hungary. This flag has been the national flag of Hungary since the middle of the 20th century.

Earth Changes

About two-thirds of the total area of Hungary is agricultural land and several areas are affected by soil erosion processes.

Soil erosion is one of the most significant land degradation processes on agricultural areas in Hungary. 25% of the total area of Hungary is affected by water erosion, 16% by wind erosion. The effect of soil erosion is very significant causing enormous damages. Erosion hazard on hill landscapes is increased loess. Loess covered areas are prone to erosion and mass movements. Wind erosion is also one of the most important land degradation processes in Hungary in the areas with low yearly precipitation values.

Population

The current population of Hungary is about 9.7 million.

Clothes

The traditional clothing of Hungary for men includes embroidered shirts, floppy hats, and pants tucked into boots made out of leather. Men also occasionally wear vests or coats, with plentiful embroidery stitched on.
 For the women of Hungary, traditional clothing consists of embellished aprons or vests, which are combined with brightly colored skirts and head-coverings made from colorful fabrics.

Food

Goulash is one national dish from Hungary. Goulash is basically a meat and potato stew but can also be made as a lighter soup. Paprika, onions, garlic, and pepper flavor the dish. It is a tasty dish. You must try it!

People

Hungarians are artistic people. They give wide recognition to talent and appreciate talent, humor, knowledge and sensitivity. Hungarians are hardworking and very intelligent people in many areas.
 The Hungarian language is a unique, complex and wonderful language and it is spoken in only one tiny country. It is a superstar language that has survived generations of attempts to make it disappear.
