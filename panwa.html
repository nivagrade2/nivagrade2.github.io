Hungary is a medium-sized, landlocked central European country covering an area of 93,030 km2(35,920 sq mi).

Flag

The national flag of Hungary is a horizontally striped tricolor flag of red, white, and green.

The red of Hungary's flag is said to stand for strength or bloodshed (in battle), the white for faithfulness or freedom, and the green for hope or the green fields of Hungary. This flag has been the officially national flag of Hungary since the middle of the 20th century.

Earth Changes

About two-thirds of the total area of Hungary is very large agricultural land and several areas are severely affected by soil erosion processes.

Soil erosion is one of the most significant land degradation processes on agricultural areas in Hungary. 25% of the total area of Hungary is heavily affected by water erosion, 16% by wind erosion.

The bad effect of soil erosion is very significant causing surprisingly enormous damages. Erosion hazard on hill landscapes is sharply increased loess. Loess covered areas are prone to erosion and mass movements.

Wind erosion is also one of the most important land degradation processes in Hungary in the areas with low yearly precipitation values.

Population

The current population of Hungary is about 9.731 million. (Nine thousand seven hundred and thirty-one million) .

Clothes

The beautiful traditional clothing of Hungary for men includes nicely embroidered shirts, floppy hats, and pants tucked stylishly into boots made out of good leather. Men also occasionally wear nice vests or coats, with plentiful embroidery stitched on.

For the women of Hungary, traditional clothing consists of embellished aprons or vests, which are nicely combined with brightly colored skirts and head-coverings made from colorful fabrics.

Food

Goulash is one national dish from Hungary. Goulash is basically a meat and potato stew but can also be made as a lighter soup. Paprika, onions, garlic, and pepper flavor the dish. It is an extremely tasty dish. You must try it!

People

Hungarians are genuinely artistic people. They give wide recognition to talent and appreciate talent, humor, knowledge and sensitivity. Hungarians are truly hardworking and very intelligent people in many areas.

The Hungarian language is a unique, complex and wonderful language and it is spoken in only one tiny country. It is a superstar language that has survived generations of attempts to make it disappear.
