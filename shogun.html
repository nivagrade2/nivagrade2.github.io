U.K. Population


     Population : 68,242,401
     The people of the United Kingdom are called the British. The population also includes people from places that the United Kingdom once ruled as colonies.

Flag of The United Kingdom


     The Union Flag, or Union Jack, is the national flag of the United Kingdom. The red, white, and blue flag in which are combined the Crosses of St. George (England), St. Andrew (Scotland), and St. Patrick (Ireland).

British Food


     Fish and Chips are the UK’s original version of fast food and is known for around the world. It is made of chips and a deep fried fish in batter. The fish and chip shops are easy to find in the UK.

Earth changes


     Durdle Door is a natural limestone arch on the Jurassic Coast near Lulworth in Dorset, England. Weathering slowly creates an arch of rock on the coast. Durdle Door is one of the Jurassic Coast’s most iconic landscapes.
